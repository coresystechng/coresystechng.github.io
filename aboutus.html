Brief Introduction

Our journey began in 2013 with the objective of providing digital solutions to individuals and organizations. With over 10 years of experience, our area of expertise cover areas such as Web Development, Graphics Design, and IT training amongst others.

What Drives Us

Our mission at CORE-TECH is to bridge the gap between people and technology through simplified, practical solutions. We are passionate about helping our community gain digital literacy and technical skills that are essential for the future. With a focus on trust, innovation, and excellence, we continue to grow as a tech partner you can count on—whether you’re a student, a startup, or an enterprise looking for a digital edge.

Our Vision

To become a leading catalyst for digital transformation across Africa—enabling individuals and organizations to harness the power of technology for growth, education, and sustainability. We envision a future where tech skills are accessible to all and innovation drives inclusive progress.
